## Color Palette Suggestions

- **B (Black)**: #000000
- **W (White)**: #FFFFFF  
- **G (Gray)**: #808080
- **D (Dark Gray)**: #404040
- **S (Silver)**: #C0C0C0
- **R (Red)**: #FF0000
- **O (Orange)**: #FF8000
- **Y (Yellow)**: #FFFF00
- **B (Blue)**: #0080FF
- **L (Light Blue)**: #80C0FF
- **G (Green)**: #00FF00
- **P (Purple)**: #8000FF
- **B (Brown)**: #8B4513

## Notes

- All rocket parts are designed to snap together on a 8-pixel grid
- Buildings are larger (32x32) for visual impact in space center
- Effects are animated - thrust flame should have 2-3 frames
- Celestial body sprites are only for icons/zoomed-out views
- UI elements are small (8x8) to save screen space
- Use transparency for empty pixels (represented by dots)
